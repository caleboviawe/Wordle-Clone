Wordle

How to Play:

  • Begin by typing some letters on your keyboard as your guess.
  • Press BACKSPACE to remove letters.
  • Press ENTER/RETURN to submit an answer.
  • Letters in the right position to the answer are in green.
  • Letters that exist in the word but are in the wrong spot are in yellow.
  • Letters that aren't in the word are in grey.
  • You have only 4 guesses. Good luck!

Additional Information:

  • Need a hint? Click the ? icon.
  • If dark mode is easier on the eye, click the ◑ icon.
